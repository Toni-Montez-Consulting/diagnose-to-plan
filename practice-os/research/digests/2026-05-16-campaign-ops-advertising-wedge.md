# Campaign Ops Advertising Wedge Research - 2026-05-16

## Purpose

Capture the advertising angle without turning the practice into a generic ad
agency, Meta media-buying service, or automated ad-spend platform.

The current best wedge is Campaign Ops: help owner-led businesses connect the
offer, landing page, creative testing, tracking, CRM follow-up, and weekly
learning loop before they spend more money on ads.

## Source Read

### Motion 2026 Creative Benchmarks

Motion's report is useful because it frames paid social as a creative testing
and operating-system problem, not a prediction problem.

Source facts checked:

- Motion analyzed more than 550,000 ads from more than 6,000 advertisers,
  representing roughly $1.3B in Facebook and Instagram spend.
- Motion says winning ads are rare: roughly 5-8% become real winners, and
  about half never get meaningful spend.
- Motion's practical guidance is to benchmark creative volume, portfolio
  breadth, winner concentration, format diversity, hook/headline diversity, and
  asset types against actual spend tier and vertical.
- Motion emphasizes that benchmark transfer should stay contextual. Vertical,
  spend tier, timing, team capacity, and current creative mix matter.

Useful transfer:

- Small businesses should not treat one failed ad as proof the offer cannot
  work.
- The work is to create a survivable testing rhythm and learn from winners,
  not to chase a magic creative.
- Creative briefs should become operating artifacts: offer, audience, format,
  hook, landing page, tracking, follow-up, owner, and learning objective.

Do not transfer blindly:

- Do not use Motion's benchmark numbers as promises for a small client.
- Do not claim top-tier testing volume is realistic for a client without the
  production capacity to support it.
- Do not imply Motion endorses Toni, DTP, or the consulting practice.

### Meta Andromeda And Advantage+ Direction

Meta's own engineering write-up says Andromeda is a personalized ads retrieval
engine that selects ads from tens of millions of candidates into a smaller set
for ranking. Meta also describes Advantage+ as increasing eligible ads through
automation of audience creation, budget allocation, dynamic placement, and
creative generation.

Source-backed implications:

- The platform is increasingly built for broad automation and many eligible
  creative variants.
- Creative diversity and signal quality matter more than narrow manual
  audience guessing.
- A client with weak tracking, unclear conversion events, or no follow-up
  workflow is not ready to scale paid ads responsibly.

### Meta Measurement And Lead Flow

Meta's Conversions API documentation frames server, website, app, CRM, offline,
and messaging events as a way to create a more reliable connection between
marketing data and Meta's optimization and measurement systems.

Meta's lead-ad materials also point to CRM connection and faster follow-up as
part of a better lead-generation workflow.

Useful transfer:

- Campaign readiness is not just creative. It includes tracking, event quality,
  CRM capture, response speed, lead qualification, and source attribution.
- For service and founder-led businesses, the first campaign failure mode may
  be lead handling, not ad creative.

## Practice Fit

This fits Toni's builder-led consulting model when positioned as:

- a paid readiness audit before ad spend;
- a Campaign Ops setup tied to a real landing page, tracking, CRM, and follow-up
  workflow;
- a weekly learning loop after launch.

It does not fit when positioned as:

- "I run your ads";
- a guaranteed ROAS service;
- an automated ad-publishing tool;
- a Motion clone;
- a generic AI ad generator;
- an agency-style media buying offer without owning the operating system around
  the campaign.

## Recommended Offer Shape

### 1. Paid Growth Readiness Audit

Use when a business is thinking about ads but may not be ready.

Outputs:

- offer and audience hypothesis;
- landing page readiness;
- tracking and conversion-event gaps;
- CRM/follow-up gaps;
- creative testing plan;
- budget guardrails;
- first 30-day test recommendation.

### 2. Campaign Ops Setup

Use when a business is ready to run a small paid test.

Outputs:

- campaign brief;
- landing page or route check;
- UTM/source tracking;
- CRM/source fields;
- lead follow-up workflow;
- creative brief backlog;
- launch checklist;
- weekly dashboard or review note.

### 3. Campaign Learning Retainer

Use only after the first campaign has real signal.

Outputs:

- weekly performance review;
- creative backlog;
- landing page iteration recommendations;
- lead quality review;
- follow-up workflow fixes;
- next test decision.
