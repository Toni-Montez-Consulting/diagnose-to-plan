## Summary Read

The AI Governance Operating Loop pattern is useful on Cam. It works because the
problem is not "add AI." The problem is keeping a prototype, data boundary,
verification story, repo-access decision, and review loop in clear lanes while
the product idea is still being shaped.

This strengthens the practice thesis:

> The model is the engine. The system around the model is the business value.

## What The Review Clarified

| Area | Sanitized read |
