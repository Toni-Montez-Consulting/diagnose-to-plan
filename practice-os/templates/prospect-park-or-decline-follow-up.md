Thanks for reaching out and sharing the context around [specific problem or
goal].

My honest read is that this may not be ready for an implementation scope yet.
The work would benefit from a bit more clarity around [missing decision, owner,
inputs, budget, timing, access, or goal] before I could responsibly recommend a
Blueprint or build path.

## Draft Variant: Decline

Subject:

Hi [Name],

Thanks for reaching out and sharing the context around [specific problem or
goal].

After reviewing it, I do not think I am the right fit for this scope. I try to
be careful about only taking on work where I can bring the right mix of
implementation judgment, technical ownership, and business value.

I appreciate you thinking of me, and I hope the project moves forward well.

Best,

Toni
