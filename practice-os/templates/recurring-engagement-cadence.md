## Notion Mirror Contract

- Source of truth: DTP private kit
- Allowed Notion fields:
  - `next_meeting`
  - `waiting_on`
  - `next_action`
  - `blocked_by`
  - `last_updated`
- Do not mirror:
  - raw transcripts;
  - private terms;
  - confidential client, employer, or project context;
  - secrets;
  - payment/member/form records;
  - unsupported proof claims.
