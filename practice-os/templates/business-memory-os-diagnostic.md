## Classification Rules

- Search only: AI can help find the answer, but no external action is taken.
- Draft only: AI can draft a response, but a human reviews and sends.
- Workflow: a follow-up task, update, reminder, or system action may happen
  after review.
- Do not automate: human judgment, sensitive facts, legal/financial/medical
  stakes, or trust risk require the owner/team to handle it.
