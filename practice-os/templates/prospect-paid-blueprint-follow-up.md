## Draft

Subject:

Hi [Name],

Thanks for the detail here. Based on what you shared, I would not want to jump
straight to a build quote yet. There is enough signal that the work could be
valuable, but the responsible first step is to turn the problem into a clear
implementation path.

The path I would recommend is a paid Blueprint. The Blueprint is a focused
diagnostic and planning sprint that produces the practical material needed to
make a good build decision:

- what the workflow or system needs to do;
- where the business value is;
- what should be built first;
- what can wait;
- what risks, access needs, or dependencies matter;
- what a realistic implementation scope could look like.

At the end, you would have a roadmap and recommended next scope instead of a
strategy deck that goes nowhere.
