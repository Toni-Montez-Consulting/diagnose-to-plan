## Capture Rule

Before updating durable docs, prompts, templates, site copy, or public language:

1. restate the remaining locks;
2. identify what is now locked vs still open;
3. ask at most three useful clarifying questions if needed;
4. produce a concise draft;
5. only then commit the durable capture when Toni approves or the direction is
   clearly settled.

Do not make Toni re-list prior items from the same thread before continuing.
