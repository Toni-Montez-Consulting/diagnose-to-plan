## Later ROI Closeout

Do not invent ROI in V0. After a real delivery cycle, capture:

- time saved;
- errors avoided;
- revenue or pipeline influenced;
- launch/proof trust gained;
- maintenance burden reduced;
- client/operator feedback;
- evidence source for each claim.
