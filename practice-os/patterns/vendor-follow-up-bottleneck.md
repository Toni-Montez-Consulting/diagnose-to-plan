# Pattern: Vendor Follow-Up Bottleneck

## Signal

The owner repeatedly has to remember who owes what, which vendor is late, or which next action is waiting.

## Real Constraint

No trusted follow-up ledger and no weekly owner-visible summary.

## Non-AI Fix First

List vendors, recurring commitments, owner approval rules, and escalation paths.

## Intervention

Weekly owner brief, draft follow-up messages, and a runbook for updating the ledger.

## Eval

Primary: overdue vendor follow-ups. Secondary: owner admin time spent reconstructing status.
