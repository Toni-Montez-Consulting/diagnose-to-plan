# Pattern: Proposal Turnaround Bottleneck

## Signal

Qualified opportunities stall because scope, price, and next steps are rebuilt from scratch.

## Real Constraint

Weak diagnose notes, fuzzy exclusions, and no standard handoff promise.

## Non-AI Fix First

Define offer bands, required inputs, exclusions, approval gates, and a reusable SOW structure.

## Intervention

Diagnose memo, plan roadmap, proposal-draft Skill, and claim/evidence discipline for proof.

## Eval

Primary: time from qualified call to proposal draft. Secondary: number of proposal revisions caused by unclear scope.
