# One-Page Explainer: Business Brain / Consulting OS

## What I Do

I help owner-led businesses install the system their business is already trying
to become.

That can mean an admin portal, intake flow, AI assistant, operating kit,
handoff manual, proposal process, or launch sprint. The tool is not the point.
The point is that the business stops depending on one person's memory.

## Who It Is For

- Small business owners carrying too much day-to-day work.
- Family operators stepping into admin roles.
- Founders with a real product but unclear onboarding or positioning.
- Professionals who need scoped build help without vague co-founder
  commitments.
- Operators who want a system they can run after the build.

## The Problem I Usually Find

The business has work happening everywhere: texts, memory, spreadsheets,
manual follow-up, half-used tools, and one person who knows how it all fits.
Adding AI on top of that does not fix the business. The first job is to find
the bottleneck and decide what is worth systematizing.

## What I Build Or Hand Off

- Diagnostic note.
- Workflow map.
- Admin surface or intake path.
- Assistant or automation where useful.
- Operator runbook.
- Weekly/close rhythm.
- Proof and handoff packet.

## What Makes This Different

The practice is repo-centered. The Business Brain stores the methods, skills,
command contracts, lessons, fixtures, proof gates, and runbooks. Each engagement
improves the system, but humans approve what changes.

It is not SaaS. It is not a course. It is not an AI automation shop. It is
business judgment plus implementation plus handoff.

## What A First Conversation Looks Like

We talk through what is messy, what breaks, what you have already tried, and
what the business needs to run without you carrying everything. The first useful
output is a clear read: the bottleneck, the first system to build, what stays
manual, and what not to do yet.
