## Pilot Readout

What the schema does well:

- Forces separation between relationship warmth and actual opportunity quality.
- Makes capacity risk visible before enthusiasm takes over.
- Gives a non-salesy next touch shape.
- Keeps high-theoretical opportunities parked until the trust path is real.
- Protects against turning every interesting operator into an active pursuit.

What needs refinement:

- A real private store decision is still needed before entering real people.
- Scores should stay advisory; relationship nuance will not fit neatly in a table.
- "Budget/readiness" should often be unknown until a real conversation happens.
- Notion mirror should show bands and next touch, not private reasoning.

Recommendation:

Use these five archetypes as the first Notion mirror design test. Do not enter
real names until the private storage and privacy boundary are chosen.
