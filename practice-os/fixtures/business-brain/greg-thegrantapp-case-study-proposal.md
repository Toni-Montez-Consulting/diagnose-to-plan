# Case-Study Proposal Fixture: Greg / TheGrantApp.io

## Situation

TheGrantApp.io has a shipped v1 and a clear market problem: businesses need a
better way to understand which grants are worth their time. The next useful work
is not rebuilding the product. It is making the value easier to understand,
making the first session more trustworthy, and helping users see why a match is
worth action.

## Recommended Scope

A focused case-study sprint on marketing-page clarity, onboarding, and
match-explanation UX.

## Deliverables

- Landing-page clarity review and rewrite direction.
- Onboarding friction map.
- Match-explanation UX recommendations.
- First-session trust checklist.
- Before/after case-study packet draft.
- Handoff note with what changed, what stayed out of scope, and what to test
  next.

## Out Of Scope

- Matching algorithm design or validation.
- Grant data acquisition.
- Production engineering ownership.
- Revenue guarantees.
- Public case-study claims without evidence and approval.

## Timeline

60-90 days, scoped to a narrow adoption/trust slice.

## Success Criteria

- A new visitor can explain what the product does and who it is for.
- A new user can understand why a grant match deserves attention.
- Greg has a clearer onboarding path and a testable next product decision.
- A case-study packet exists, subject to evidence and approval.

## Access Needed

- Current landing page and product flow.
- Basic funnel or activation observations, if available.
- Permission to review onboarding screens.
- Agreement on what can and cannot be named in a case study.
