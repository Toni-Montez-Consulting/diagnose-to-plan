# Operator Handoff Fixture: Mom / Mario Pickleball Admin

## What This System Is For

This admin system helps Mario's pickleball coaching business keep track of
students, scheduling, payment/status checks, and admin follow-up. Mom is the
operator. She does not need technical architecture; she needs a clear routine.

## What To Check Every Day

- Open the admin home.
- Check for new students or leads.
- Look for schedule conflicts or missing information.
- Confirm anything urgent has a next step.

## What To Update Every Week

- Review student list.
- Confirm upcoming lessons or sessions.
- Check payment/status area for anything that needs follow-up.
- Write down questions for the weekly check-in.

## How To Handle New Students / Leads

1. Find the new lead or student.
2. Confirm name, contact method, lesson interest, and scheduling note.
3. Mark the next action.
4. If something is unclear, put it in the weekly question list instead of
   guessing.

## How To Fix Or Report Common Problems

- Missing student detail: add it to questions.
- Schedule looks wrong: do not delete anything; flag it for review.
- Payment/status looks confusing: screenshot it and ask.
- Portal seems broken: note what page you were on and what you clicked.

## Loom Scripts

### Daily Check, 5 Minutes

"Open the admin home. First look for anything new. Then check the schedule. If
something is missing, do not try to fix everything at once. Add it to the weekly
question list. The goal is to keep the business from drifting, not to become the
technical owner."

### Weekly Admin, 10 Minutes

"This is the weekly reset. Review students, schedule, and payment/status. Look
for anything that has been sitting too long. Write the questions in one place so
we can clear them in the weekly check-in."

### Three Common Break Points

"The first break point is missing information. The second is a schedule that
does not match what someone said. The third is payment or status confusion. In
all three cases, pause, take a screenshot if useful, and ask instead of changing
settings."

## Standing Weekly Check-In

- What looked confusing this week?
- Any new student or lead that needs follow-up?
- Any schedule conflict?
- Any payment/status question?
- What should be added to the manual?
- What can Mom now do without Toni?
