## Homepage Source Draft

### Hero

**Turn unclear ideas, broken workflows, and tech bottlenecks into practical
systems that work.**

I'm a builder/advisor for serious founders, operators, and small businesses. I
help clarify what needs to happen, map the business value, and implement
working digital assets, workflows, AI-enabled systems, or operating tools.

Primary CTA:

> Start With a Fit Call

Secondary CTA:

> See How The Work Starts

### Why Now / Small Team Advantage

Recommended placement: directly after the hero and before the three public
doors.

Recommended heading:

**Small teams have more leverage than ever.**

AI, cloud tools, APIs, and modern app workflows make it possible to build
systems that used to require a bigger team. But leverage does not come from
adding more tools. It comes from knowing what to build first, what to leave
alone, and how to turn the work into something the business can actually run.

That is where I help: clarify the bottleneck, sequence the work, build the
useful version, and hand it off cleanly.

Use `docs/PRACTICE_HOMEPAGE_SMALL_TEAM_ADVANTAGE_COPY_BRIEF.md` as the source
for alternate copy, public boundaries, and implementation guidance.

### Three Doors

#### Clarify The Path

For ideas, messy workflows, unclear scope, or business problems that need to
become a real plan.

If you know something needs to move but you are not sure what to build first,
we start by defining the business problem, the value of solving it, the
workflow behind it, and the smartest next step.

Likely route: Fit Call -> Blueprint.

#### Build The System

For sites, apps, workflows, dashboards, AI-enabled tools, admin systems, or
operating assets that need to exist and work.

If the direction is clear enough to move, I help design and build the practical
system: the thing your business can use, hand off, measure, and improve.

Likely route: Fast Track or Custom Implementation SOW.

#### Improve The System

For existing businesses that need less manual drag, better processes, smarter
tools, or practical AI/workflow upgrades.

If the business already works but the operation feels scattered, slow, or too
dependent on memory, we find the friction and improve the operating path.

Likely route: Blueprint -> Implementation -> optional advisory.

### Blueprint Bridge

**Bigger or unclear work starts with a Blueprint.**

A Blueprint is a paid diagnostic that turns messy context into a practical
roadmap: business value case, workflow map, technical plan, risks, scope
options, and an implementation recommendation.

It is how we decide what is worth building before you spend serious
implementation money.

Link:

> See a Sample Blueprint

### Implementation Promise

**No strategy deck dead-end.**

The goal is not to leave you with a pile of advice. Every paid engagement
should move toward something tangible: a roadmap, workflow map, working site,
app, intake system, dashboard, admin flow, AI-assisted process, handoff guide,
or operating asset your business can actually use.

AI is used where it creates leverage: organizing context, reducing manual drag,
supporting follow-up, improving workflows, or helping implementation move
faster. Human review and business judgment stay built into the process.

### Proof Cards

#### Omnexus

Product/platform proof.

Omnexus shows the product side of the practice: app workflows,
subscription/billing trust, launch repair, release readiness, and the
discipline required to move a real digital product through messy constraints.

#### DeMario

Local business implementation proof.

DeMario shows the local-operator side: booking, payment flow, availability,
owner admin, launch handoff, and practical service-business systems that a real
operator can use.

#### Consulting System / DTP

Operating discipline proof.

My own consulting system shows the method behind the work: intake, triage,
follow-up, proof gates, roadmap decisions, workflow mapping, and durable
operating assets instead of loose notes and scattered execution.

### Fit Filter

This is a good fit if you are serious about moving something real:

- you have an idea but need a path;
- your current workflow is messy or too manual;
- you need a site, app, tool, or operating asset built;
- you want to use AI where it actually helps;
- your business needs clearer systems before it can grow.

This is probably not a fit for cheap one-off websites, random automations with
no operating model, chatbot requests for the sake of having a chatbot, vague
strategy-only work, or projects with no urgency, budget, or real owner
commitment.

### Final CTA

Start with a Fit Call.

Bring the messy context. We'll sort out whether the right next step is a
Blueprint, Fast Track implementation, a custom SOW, advisory support, or not
building yet.

## `/start` Triage Lock

The `/start` page should feel like a calm first sorting step, not a giant
intake form.

It should answer:

> Tell me what you're trying to move, what's stuck, and whether this is worth a
> serious conversation.

Latest decision: `/start` is the client-clarity surface before stronger proof
or conversion work. It should help the visitor understand what the Fit Call
decides: whether the next move is a Blueprint, Fast Track implementation,
Custom Implementation SOW, advisory path, simpler fix, or deliberate pause.
The page should make "not building yet" feel like legitimate professional
judgment, not a failure to sell.

Use these questions:

1. **What are you trying to move forward?**
   - An idea I need help shaping.
   - A website, app, workflow, or system I need built.
   - An existing process that is messy or too manual.
   - An AI/workflow opportunity I do not know how to approach.
   - A business/project that is stuck and needs a clearer path.

2. **Which door sounds closest?**
   - Clarify The Path.
   - Build The System.
   - Improve The System.
   - I am not sure yet.

3. **What is currently causing the most friction?**
   - I do not know what to build first.
   - My current tools/process are scattered.
   - Leads, clients, or tasks are falling through cracks.
   - I need technical help to get something working.
   - I want to use AI, but only if it actually helps.
   - Other.

4. **What already exists?**
   - Just an idea.
   - A live business with manual workflows.
   - A website or app that needs improvement.
   - A partial build/prototype.
   - Existing tools/processes that need cleanup.

5. **What would make this engagement successful?**
   - A clear roadmap.
   - A working site/app/tool/workflow.
   - Less manual admin work.
   - Better intake, follow-up, or handoff.
   - Practical AI/workflow improvement.
   - A scoped implementation plan or SOW.

6. **How soon does this need to move?**
   - This week.
   - This month.
   - In the next 1-3 months.
   - I am exploring.

7. **If there is a fit, what level of investment are you prepared to consider?**
   - I am not sure yet; I need help understanding the path.
   - I am looking for a focused Blueprint or diagnostic first.
   - I am prepared for a smaller implementation if the scope is clear.
   - I am prepared for a larger build if the business case makes sense.
   - I am mainly exploring and not ready to invest yet.

8. **What context should I know before the call?**

   Free text with this privacy note: do not include passwords, payment details,
   private client records, sensitive business exports, or full dashboard
   exports.
